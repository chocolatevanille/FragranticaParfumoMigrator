Interesting Facts
The faces of the advertising campaign since 2025 are British model Theo James and Italian model Vittoria Ceretti, filmed by director Gordon von Steiner. The fragrance was awarded the "Fragrance Hall of Fame" prize by the Fragrance Foundation in 2016.

Very similar to Chanel Chance eau Tendre
Light Blue smells very similar to Chanel Chance eau Tendre but opens up with an almost spicy brightness. It’s less citrusy and a bit musky in the drydown. I’d thought I found Chance slightly off-putting because of its apple-like quince note—I dislike the scent of apples in perfumes—but Light Blue actually has an apple note, and I find it more pleasant than Chance.

A joyful summer morning on the Italian coast
Light Blue by Dolce & Gabbana is a crisp, fresh, and clean citrus notes gently accented by soft woods. The composition evokes a joyful summer morning on the Italian coast. Ethereal and delicate, it offers a subtle, safe presence for warm weather. Perfect for everyday wear when you're seeking lightness and natural freshness.
